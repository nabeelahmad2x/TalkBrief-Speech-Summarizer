University of Central Punjab

At the University of Central Punjab, we're proud to be a leading educational institution in Lahore, Pakistan. Since 2002, we've provided a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, from engineering and computer science to business and social sciences. We believe in fostering well-rounded individuals, so our students enjoy not only modern academic facilities and labs but also a vibrant campus life with sports complexes and entrepreneurship initiatives. Our business school is a particular point of pride, consistently ranking among the top in Lahore. We're also actively engaged in international collaborations, bringing fresh perspectives to our classrooms and research endeavors. If you're seeking a dynamic and enriching educational experience, we invite you to consider UCP!
